Making of Cornered!

Cornered! is a simple dice-throwing game by web designer and developer Peter Surowski. Your attacks generate a random number between 10 and 20 which is subtracted from your opponent's HP. Your opponents' attacks generate random numbers with three different ranges: 5-15, 10-20 and 10-40. You will lose most of the time.

Two versions are available on Peter's GitHub page. He coded one with plain JavaScript and the other with jQuery in order to demostrate his ability to use both. He coded both the HTML and CSS files from scratch; no libraries or frameworks such as Bootstrap.

Peter devoted about a dozen hours to the project, which was an assignment in The Coding Bootcamp at University of California, Irvine.
